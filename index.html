North India

Near the Taj Mahal, visit Agra Fort, Itimad-ud-Daulah's Tomb, and Mehtab Bagh for great views. Also check out Fatehpur Sikri, Akbar's Tomb, and the Jama Masjid.

South India

In South India, explore Mysore’s palace, Kerala’s backwaters, Hampi’s ruins, Ooty’s hill station, Kochi’s colonial charm, Madurai’s Meenakshi Temple, Kumarakom’s serene waters, and Chennai’s cultural sites.

Karnataka

Near Goa, you can visit Mumbai for its lively markets, Pune for its cultural heritage, Gokarna for tranquil beaches, Karwar for a quiet coastal retreat, and Dudhsagar Falls for stunning natural beauty.
